# Client brand access

Each end client has access to one or more brand workspaces inside their reseller's instance.

- By default, a new end client gets one brand workspace.
- The reseller can grant or restrict access to additional workspaces.
- Brand data (logos, assets, print files) is scoped to the end client and not visible to other clients.
