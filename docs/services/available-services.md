# Available services

## Brand Builder
The core BrandForge product. End clients answer a brand questionnaire and the AI generates a complete brand identity: name suggestions, logo concepts, color palette, typography, and brand voice guidelines.

**Credit cost:** 50 credits per AI Brand Setup (one-time per brand)

## Logo Generator
Standalone logo creation experience. End clients describe their business and style preferences, and the generator produces logo concepts with brand mockups (storefronts, business cards, merchandise).

**Credit cost:** 10 credits per generation

## Web-to-Print
Generates print-ready files (PDF, AI) for common collateral: business cards, letterhead, flyers, brochures, and banners — using the brand assets from Brand Builder.

**Credit cost:** 5 credits per document

## Social Media Kit
Generates a set of on-brand social media graphics in the correct dimensions for major platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, X/Twitter).

**Credit cost:** 15 credits per kit

## Site Builder
AI-assisted website builder. End clients describe their business and the AI generates a complete website structure, copy, and design — using their brand identity from Brand Builder.

**Credit cost:** 20 credits per AI-generated page

## E-commerce
Headless e-commerce storefront that integrates with the Site Builder. End clients can add products, configure payment methods, and launch an online store.

**Credit cost:** No AI credit cost — storage and transaction fees apply separately.

## Domain Registrar
Domain availability search and registration integration. Connects to domain registrar APIs to allow end clients to register or transfer domains directly within their brand portal.

**Credit cost:** Domain registration fees pass through at cost. No additional credit charge.
