# Top up credits

Credit top-ups allow resellers (or the Super Admin on their behalf) to purchase additional credits beyond their monthly package allocation. Top-up credits do not expire.

## Top up as Super Admin

1. Go to **GodMode → Billing → Top up** or **Resellers → [reseller] → Credits → Top up**.
2. Select the reseller or distributor.
3. Enter the credit amount.
4. Select the payment method or mark as manually invoiced.
5. Confirm. Credits are added immediately.

## Top up as a reseller (self-service)

Resellers can top up their own credit balance from their reseller dashboard:

1. **Reseller dashboard → Credits → + Top up**.
2. Select a top-up bundle or enter a custom amount.
3. Complete payment via the configured payment method.
4. Credits are added immediately after payment confirmation.

## Volume discounts on top-ups

Top-up purchases automatically apply the volume discount tier for the reseller's package. See [Volume discounts →](/docs/packages/volume-discounts).
