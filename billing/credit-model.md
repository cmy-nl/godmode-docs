# Credit model

## Credit pricing tiers

Credits are sold at different price points depending on the reseller's package tier and purchase volume. See [Volume discounts →](/docs/packages/volume-discounts) for the discount schedule.

## Credit consumption rates

| Action | Credits |
|---|---|
| AI Brand Setup | 50 |
| Logo generation | 10 |
| Web-to-Print document | 5 |
| Social media kit | 15 |
| Site Builder AI page | 20 |

These rates are configured in **GodMode → Services → [service] → Configure** and apply platform-wide.

## Monthly included credits

Each package includes a monthly credit allocation that resets on the reseller's billing anniversary date. Credits do not roll over — unused credits expire at the end of the billing period. Top-up credits (purchased separately) do not expire.

## Credit balance visibility

- **Super Admin** — sees total credits across the entire platform, per distributor, and per reseller
- **Distributor admin** — sees credits across their reseller pool
- **Reseller admin** — sees their own balance and can view per-end-client consumption
